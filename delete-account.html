How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: Delete from the App

  1. Open the Rostr app on your device
  2. Go to Profile (bottom navigation)
  3. Tap Settings (gear icon)
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm your decision when prompted

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app, email us with your account details:

Before You Delete

Please consider the following:

  • Active subscriptions: Cancel your subscription through Google Play or Apple App Store before deleting your account to avoid continued billing.
  • Team impact: If you're a manager/admin, transfer ownership to another user first.
  • Export data: If you need records of your time entries, request an export before deletion.

Reactivation

Once your account is deleted, it cannot be recovered. If you wish to use Rostr again in the future, you will need to create a new account.
